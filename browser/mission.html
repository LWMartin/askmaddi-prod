Ad-Free. Privacy-First.

Why we built AskMaddi differently.

"Morality, beauty, and truth over revenue or scale."

The Problem

Every time you search for a product online, you become the product. Your search history is tracked, profiled, and sold. Ads follow you across the internet. Your data feeds algorithms designed to maximize someone else's profit, not your benefit.

We believe there's a better way.

Our Approach

AskMaddi is built on a simple principle: your searches belong to you.

The intelligence that understands your query runs entirely in your browser. When you search for "wireless headphones under $100," that query is processed on your device — not our servers. We literally cannot see what you're searching for.

This isn't just a privacy policy. It's our architecture. The code itself guarantees your privacy.

Our Values

  • Privacy by design — We don't collect what we don't need. Your data stays on your device.
  • No ads, ever — We will never show you advertisements. Your attention is not for sale.
  • Transparency — We earn affiliate commissions when you buy through our links. That's it. No hidden monetization.
  • User sovereignty — You control your experience. No manipulation, no dark patterns, no algorithmic feeds designed to keep you scrolling.

How We Sustain This

When you find a product through AskMaddi and choose to buy it, we may earn a small affiliate commission from the retailer. This costs you nothing extra — it's simply how retailers reward sites that send them customers.

This model aligns our interests with yours: we succeed when we help you find what you're actually looking for, not when we manipulate you into clicking ads.

AskMaddi is part of a larger vision — building technology that serves humanity rather than exploiting it. We believe the best tools are the ones that respect the people using them.

Thank you for being here. Every search you run through AskMaddi is a vote for a different kind of internet.

— The AI Science Center Team
